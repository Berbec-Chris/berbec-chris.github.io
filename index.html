About Me

My experience spans freelance consulting in UX/UI design, architectural visualization, and event organization, equipping me with a versatile skill set that combines technical expertise with creative problem-solving. I have collaborated with businesses, independent developers, and architects, to deliver projects that align with user needs and business objectives.

  • Proficient in design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, and AutoCAD, I also have a foundational understanding of programming languages including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. My ability to analyze complex challenges, synthesize solutions, and adapt to dynamic environments makes me a valuable asset to any design team.
  • I am currently seeking opportunities where I can apply my skills in interaction design and UX/UI to contribute to innovative projects and create impactful digital experiences.
